Explore the Heart of Ireland

Dublin, the vibrant capital of Ireland, offers a unique mix of ancient history and modern culture. A city filled with museums, galleries, lively pubs, and stunning architecture.

Dublin History

Explore the rich heritage and stories that shaped Ireland's vibrant capital.

Viking Period

Dublin's history began in the 9th century when it was established as a key trade and military base by the Vikings.

Norman Conquest

In the 12th century, the Normans conquered Ireland, transforming Dublin into a significant administrative and commercial hub.

Dublin Castle

Built in the 13th century, Dublin Castle became a symbol of English rule in Ireland, cementing the city's political importance.

Renaissance and Commercial Growth

From the 16th to the 17th century, Dublin flourished under English rule as a cultural and economic center.

Independence Movement

Between the late 19th and early 20th centuries, Dublin was at the forefront of Ireland's nationalist struggle for independence.

Modern Era

In the latter half of the 20th century, Dublin grew into a modern, international metropolis while preserving its rich historical legacy.
